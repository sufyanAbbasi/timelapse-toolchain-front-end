Timelapse Visualization Generator

A simple CREATE Lab utility to allow non-programmers
to create embeddable WebGL based map visualizations.

How It Works

upload your .csv file (comma-separated values)

wait to recieve an email after processing has finished

customize and publish your visualization

Valid file layouts:

  • Lat, Lon
  • Lat, Lon, Time
  • Lat, Lon, Time, HexColor (Coming soon!)
  • Lat, Lon, Time, R, G, B, A  (Coming soon!)

Latitude and longitude must be specified in WGS84 decimal degrees.

Dates must be supplied as strings in “YYYY-MM-DD” format,
or dates with times in "YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS" or ISO 8601 "2016-01-01T09:00:00.000" format.

Columns must match the following format:

  • Latitude: first column (no header), or named “lat” or “latitude”
  • Longitude: second column (no header), or named “lon” or “longitude”
  • Time: third column (no header), or named “date” or “time”
